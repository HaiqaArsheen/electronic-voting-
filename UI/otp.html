Voting

Voting Election are a main fundamental pillar of an Pakistan democratic system enabling the Pakistan public to express their party member in the form of vote. Due to their interest to our society, the election polling process should be user friendly and easy to use. This system is primarily drived by the hard efforts to make the system secure, immutable, distributed.

Block Chain

Blockchain technology offers a decentralized node for online voting or electronic voting. Recently distributed ledger technologies such blockchain were used to produce electronic voting systems mainly because of their end-to-end verification advantages. Blockchain is an appealing alternative to conventional electronic voting systems with features such as decentralization, non-repudiation, and security protection.
